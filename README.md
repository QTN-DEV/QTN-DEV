# 👋 Welcome to Quantum Teknologi Nusantara (QTN) Developers!

## 🚀 About Us
**Quantum Teknologi Nusantara (QTN)** is an Indonesian technology company specializing in **low-code development** and **artificial intelligence (AI) solutions**. We help organizations build mission-critical systems faster and smarter by integrating **low-code platforms with AI**. Our solutions drive digital transformation across industries worldwide, including **Fortune 500 companies**.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission & Vision
### 🌟 Mission
Accelerate innovation by delivering fast, intelligent solutions through **low-code development and AI**. We empower organizations to create smart applications that transform how they operate.

### 🔮 Vision
To eliminate technology barriers, enabling organizations to **harness AI and modern development tools** effortlessly. QTN strives to be a **leading force in AI-driven solutions**, revolutionizing industries and improving lives.
